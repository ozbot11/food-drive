--- About us: ---


Food & Drive is a nonprofit organization whose aim is to link restaurant to homeless shelters.
Our mission is to better the Seattle area by getting surplus food to people in need of food.
In addition we provide restaurant with an analytical report on their food supply and excess in order to help prevent the mass wasting of food.
Registering is completly free for restaurant, volunteers, and homeless shelters.

--- Restaurants: ---


Restaurants can register on our site and apply to get their excess food shipped to a homeless shelter by our volunteers.
Restaurants will also get a detailed report with analytics helping them to determine how to waste less food.

--- Volunteers: ---


Volunteers can register on our site to help transport the surplus food from the restaurant to the homeless shelters.
Volunteer hours will be awarded to people who register and help out our organization.

--- Homeless Shelters: ---


Homeless shelters can register on our site to receive shipments of excess food from registered restaurant.
